IO Test

This site is designed to help you check the functionality and quality of various hardware components of your computer. With these tools, you can test your computer's display, keyboard, webcam, speakers, and microphone to ensure that they are functioning correctly. Each test has a brief description of what it is designed to check and how to use it. Simply follow the instructions provided in each section to run the test.

Keyboard Test

Description: The keyboard test is designed to verify that all the keys on the laptop's keyboard are functioning correctly. It will visually indicate when a key is pressed, allowing you to ensure that each key is registering correctly.

How to use: Press each key on your laptop's keyboard. When a key is pressed, it will be highlighted on the virtual keyboard displayed on the screen. Ensure that all keys are registering correctly and that there are no issues with key responsiveness or key mapping.

Webcam Test

Description: The webcam test is designed to check the functionality and quality of the laptop's built-in webcam. It will display the camera feed, allowing you to ensure that the webcam is functioning properly and producing a clear image.

How to use: Click the "Start Webcam Test" button, and the webcam feed will be displayed on the screen. Ensure that the image is clear and that there are no issues with the camera, such as distortion, poor color reproduction, or other visual problems.

Speaker Test

Description: The speaker test is designed to check the quality and functionality of the laptop's built-in speakers. It will play a series of audio clips to help you identify any potential issues with the speakers, such as poor audio quality, distortion, or lack of volume.

How to use: Click the "Start Speaker Test" button, and the test will play a series of audio clips through the laptop's speakers. Listen carefully for any issues with the audio quality, volume, or distortion.

Microphone Test

Description: The microphone test is designed to check the functionality and quality of the laptop's built-in microphone. It will record a short audio clip and play it back, allowing you to ensure that the microphone is functioning properly and producing clear audio.

How to use: Click the "Start Microphone Test" button, and the test will begin recording a 5-second audio clip using the laptop's built-in microphone and display a waveform of the sound. After the recording is complete, the test will play back the audio. Listen carefully for any issues with audio quality, volume, or distortion.
